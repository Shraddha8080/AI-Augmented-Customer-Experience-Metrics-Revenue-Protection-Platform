## 📌 Executive Summary
Operational drag, unhandled logistics friction, and poor customer service resolution lead to severe **revenue leakage** in e-commerce environments. This project delivers an end-to-end analytical framework that:
1. **Engineers relational database queries (SQL)** to isolate operational bottlenecks and quantify direct financial risk.
2. **Visualizes executive diagnostics (Power BI)** to track complaint volume, affected revenue, and category-level drag.
3. **Architects a Generative AI text-processing workflow (LLMs)** to convert unstructured, multi-lingual customer feedback into structured root-cause audits and actionable engineering tickets.

---

## 🛠️ System Architecture & Tech Stack
* **Data Processing & ETL:** SQL (MySQL / PostgreSQL) for aggregation, filtering, and metric calculation.
* **Business Intelligence & Dashboards:** Microsoft Power BI (DAX, Funnel Analysis, Matrix Views, Executive Layouts).
* **Generative AI Engine:** LLM-driven prompt engineering (Gemini / ChatGPT) for sentiment auditing, root-cause clustering, and automated JIRA ticket generation.
* **Data Visualizations & Auditing:** Python (`pandas`, `matplotlib`, `seaborn`) for exploratory audit and summary visualization.

---

## 📊 Key Findings & Business Impact
* **Total Quantified Financial Risk:** Identified over **₹16.98 Lakhs ($142.5K+)** in at-risk revenue across 710 customer complaints.
* **Primary Bottleneck Identified:** `Order Related` delays and `Returns - Technician Visit` timeouts account for over **78%** of total revenue risk.
* **Operational Friction Discovery:** Database logs showed `0-minute` resolution times due to dropped chat sessions and unhandled 3PL dispatch timeouts, hiding true resolution times of 10–14 days.
